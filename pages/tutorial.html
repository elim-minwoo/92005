How To
RETRIEVE

In every level, you are able to maneuver your cart around the map to collect and move valuables more easily. Because you can only hold one valuable at once, it is important to not lose your cart or damage it, as everything inside it will also lose value... or even worse, be destroyed.

Once you put in your objects within the cart, it will show the total value of the items inside it; helping you keep track of the money you have at the moment, and how much more you need to achieve your goal.

How To
EXTRACT

When you think you reached your goal, you can always find your way to the nearest extraction point and extract your money. When you finish, the money will be deposited to be used in the shop, the rest remaining as cash for the next extraction point.

As you reach higher levels, you required extraction points will grow. You would first start with only one required, which will increase as you elevate into new heights.

But be fast, as the sound of your last extraction point makes a loud noise towards you and your ship... causing nearby monsters to swarm towards you, it's numbers increasing over time.

How To
PROFIT

So you've passed the level; now what? Using the money that you earned, you can now heal, upgrade, and gear up to reorganize into new levels. Increased extraction points, more monsters, and tougher rooms will await your arrival!

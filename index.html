RETRIEVE.

You and up to 5 friends must team up and explore through horrifying environments, where ominous entities lurk within rooms.

It is your job to locate, transport and retrieve valuable items from the haunted remains of a long lost humanity. Will you be able to retrieve enough valuables to satisfy your quota, let alone survive?

EXTRACT.

Transport anything from heavy pianos to fragile ceramics and use teamwork to make sure that the precious cargo will safely reach its destination.

Remember; you will encounter dangerous creatures in the process, threatening you and your valuables. One slip, and your precious objects would be lost forever...

PROFIT.

Use the money that you earned to upgrade, enhance , and fight back enemies... for more money! More upgrades, tools, and most of all, weapons! Between each level, you will be able to access a shop where you can buy different things to prepare for your next venture.

With more ways to fight back against your enemies, you and your team are able to proceed through new stages, your quota increasing level by level.
